Brand Identity

Four distinct brand identities — each built from the ground up with a unique visual language, tone, and design system tailored to the brand's products and audience.

01

Tera House

Tableware & Lifestyle

Tera House is a minimal tableware brand rooted in the idea that everyday objects deserve exceptional design. The identity uses clean geometric forms, a restrained material palette, and quiet typography to elevate the dining ritual — from morning coffee cups to curated table settings. Every touchpoint whispers quality.

STRKR

Apparel & Streetwear

STRKR is a streetwear brand built for those who move with intention. The identity draws from urban culture, sport, and raw energy — bold wordmarks, oversized graphics, and a visual system that works as hard as the people wearing it. T-shirts, caps, and shorts carry the brand's attitude without saying a word.

Floww

App UI / UX

Floww is a productivity app built around the idea that good design removes friction. The UI/UX identity is obsessively clean — every screen, interaction, and micro-animation designed to guide the user without distraction. The brand language mirrors the product: fluid, focused, and purposeful. A case study in designing for clarity.

Deskraft

Desk & Stationery

Deskraft is a premium desk lifestyle brand for creative professionals. Every object in the collection — from ceramic cups to weighted pens — is designed with the same level of care a designer gives their work.
